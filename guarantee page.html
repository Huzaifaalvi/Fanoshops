Lifetime Guarantee

* Lifetime Guarantee is valid up to 18 years from the date of purchase.

* Guarantee is Valid only if the fan stops working due to burning of motor. In * such cases only the Motor will be replaced.

* If the fan stops working due to any other reason such as capacitor or bush failure within one year of the date of purchase, then the fan will be repaired Free of Charge. After One (1) Year, it will be repaired on Buyer’s Expense.

* In case of claim, the fan will be replaced as per the following procedure:

* Within one year from the Date of purchase — By the dealer from whom it was purchased.

* During the next four years — by the concerned Distributor or Sales Office.

* After five and up to the validity of the guarantee — by the factory.

* Expenses for sending the fan to the dealer, distributor or factory and receiving replacement will be borne by the customer.

* Guarantee does not cover normal wear and tear.
